OUR MISSION:

Biomanufacturing has the potential to replace the worlds petrochemical-derived products with renewable biobased chemicals and materials. The production of these biobased products requires novel tools which can effectively and  efficiently convert biological material into (bio)chemical building blocks which can feed into industries across the globe.

At level nine, we are building next generation catalysts to unlock this potential of biomanufacturing and create the chemicals industry of the future.

TEAM:

We are hiring a team that is passionate about building a sustainable future through novel innovation.

Our technology is being built at the cutting edge, drawing from knowledge across many disciplines including:

  1. Catalysis 
  2. Computational Chemistry/Molecular Modelling 
  3. Analytical/Process Chemistry
  4. Biochemistry 
  5. Protein Engineering 
  6. Hybrid Nanotechnology
